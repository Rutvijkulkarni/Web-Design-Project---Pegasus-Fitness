The Honcho

Conor McGregor



Pegasus Fitness, was the UFC Lightweight and former Featherweight champion's vision. His objective has always been to keep the Irish people fit and healthy and thus introduced a fitness centre in the heart of the city where he was born.





The Gym Team


Before any trainer joins our gym team, we make sure that they pass the Certificate in strength and conditioning, thus making sure that what our trainers teach is uniform and professional.


The Aqua Team


100 metres freestyle in 30 seconds, is the record that you need to break to join the aqua team at pegasus. We also make sure that every trainer is registered with SwimIreland.
